The Design Process

Discovery

We start with a brief consultation to understand your brand, audience, goals, and vision. No guesswork — just clarity.

Strategy

I research your market and competitors, then develop a design strategy that positions you to stand out.

Design

Concepts are crafted with intention. You receive 2–3 initial directions with full rationale, then we refine together.

Delivery

All files delivered in your required formats — print, web, social — along with usage guidelines. Done.
